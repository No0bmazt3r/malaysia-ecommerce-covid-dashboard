# Presentation & Demonstration Script

**Slide 1: Title Screen**
"Good morning, panel. My name is Sharvin, and today I will be presenting the Malaysia E-Commerce COVID-19 Diagnostic Dashboard. This is not just a reporting tool; it's an algorithmic, zero-latency diagnostic engine built for supply chain and marketing executives."

**Slide 2: The Problem (Problem Definition & Objectives)**
"When the pandemic hit, e-commerce networks broke down. Our objective was to give the CMO and Logistics Director a way to instantly identify where supply chain bottlenecks were destroying SLAs by >15%, and where localized ad spend could be optimized to improve ROAS by >20%. To do this, we analyzed 3,500 transactional records across four specific MCO phases."

**Slide 3: User-Centered Design (Accessibility)**
"We designed this with three strict accessibility profiles:
1. **The Data Analyst**: Standard dense mode.
2. **The Elderly Mode**: Built to WCAG 2.5.5 standards, forcing tap targets to ≥44px, scaling typography to 18px, and ensuring 5:1 contrast to combat aging lens scatter.
3. **The Early Childhood Mode**: Complex filters are stripped. We implemented a scientifically validated colorblind-safe traffic-light palette, and strictly enforced a massive 64x64px minimum hit-area for developing motor skills."

**Slide 4: Advanced Visualizations (Heat Map & SPLOM)**
*(Demonstrate Heat Map in dashboard)*
"A standard grouped bar chart with 14 states and 4 phases would require 56 unreadable bars. Our Heat Map condenses this perfectly. Look here at the 'Sabah Anomaly'—while peninsular sales dropped during MCO, Sabah's revenue spiked massively."

*(Demonstrate Scatter Matrix)*
"Next, instead of a boring correlation table that hides outliers, our 6x6 Scatter Plot Matrix visually proves a strong negative correlation (r = -0.75) between Delivery Time and Customer Rating strictly during the MCO phase. We mathematically proved logistics failures caused churn, not product quality."

**Slide 5: Generative BI (The Insights Engine)**
"Most dashboards force you to interpret the charts yourself. Ours has a built-in Generative BI engine. As I click on 'Groceries', watch the Insights panel. Our custom D3-array hook recalculates Pearson correlations and Z-scores deterministically in under 5 milliseconds—no API keys required—and generates prescriptive, causal business strategies right on the screen."

**Slide 6: Project Timeline**
"We tracked the entire academic lifecycle in an interactive timeline, plotting the 5 full months from Project Initiation in May all the way through to Deployment in late October. The timeline also visually maps directly to the real-world COVID phases below it for context."

**Slide 7: Conclusion & Q&A**
"This dashboard is heavily optimized: SSR code-splitting, a custom deduplication engine for our multi-token debounced search, and zero third-party chart wrappers—just pure D3 math. We are ready to take your questions."
